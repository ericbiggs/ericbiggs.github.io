Linguistics

I specialize in cognitve semantics. My focus has been the modeling of multiple cognitive semantic theories in synthetic speech agents.

With my specialty of cognitive semantics, I also deliver analysis on issues of speech acts, the environments they occur in, and their differing cues (including gestures and intonation) to industry leaders and their teams.

Cognitive Modeling

I model cognition in various ways. I develop artificially intelligent language models that are capable of performing language tasks with humans.

I build cognitive models that can understand natural language (NLU) as well as generate natural language (NLG).

Natural Language Processing

I also process natural language where I mine textual data and develop natural language processing (NLP) algorithms.

I proccess this data in order to tease out many insights. Among these insights are sentiment analysis and topic modeling, which provide significant understanding into how, what, and why language has been used.
